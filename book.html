Booking FAQ

Common questions.

How far in advance should I book?
Forty-eight hours is the comfortable minimum. For overnights and longer stays, give me a week or more if you can — it lets me lock the schedule and arrange family backup. Last-minute is fine if I'm available; just text and ask.
What's the meet & greet like?
About twenty minutes at your place. We sit on the floor with your pet, you walk me through their routine, I ask questions, you ask questions, no pressure to book. The point is for everyone — including the pet — to know what they're getting into.
Can I cancel?
Yes. Cancellations 48+ hours ahead get a full refund. Within 48 hours, the deposit is non-refundable but I can usually rebook you within the same week.
How does payment work?
Half upfront when you book, half on completion. Cash, Zelle, or check — your call. No platform fees, no surprise charges.
Will you take a pet you've never met for an overnight?
No — overnights always require a meet & greet first. For walks and short day visits, sometimes I can skip the greet if your situation is straightforward, but I prefer the hello.
What if my pet has medical needs?
Tell me everything at the meet & greet — meds, schedule, vet contact, what counts as a real emergency. I've handled insulin, daily pills, anxiety meds, and senior-pet care. Bring written instructions from your vet.
Can I get updates while I'm away?
Always. Photo updates every visit are part of every booking. For longer overnights, you'll also get a quick text summary of how the day went.
